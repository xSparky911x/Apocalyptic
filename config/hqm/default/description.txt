100 years after the humans have made Earth completely uninhabitable from nuclear war, you have been chosen to be sent back down to the surface from the failing space where you were born to see if Earth can once again be populated. The re-entry into the atmosphere didn't go as planned and some of your equipment has been destroyed. Unable to communicate with any of your comrades you are all alone on the surface of what might be, your death sentence... or at least you think you are alone...
